About Me

As a recent graduate from California State University with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, I have transitioned seamlessly into the world of datacenter operations at i3D.net. My academic journey, bolstered by an Associate of Science in Computer Network & Security Management, has equipped me with a robust foundation in hardware troubleshooting and network services. At i3D.net, our team supports global datacenter hardware, ensuring operational excellence through maintenance and complex problem-solving. My role includes collaborating with internal and external partners to deliver reliable and efficient solutions, reflecting my commitment to continuous learning and passion for technology infrastructure. I work part time as a Teacher and member of the Oversight Team at CodeFu in San Fernando Valley. In my free time, I create projects using Java, HTML/CSS, JavaScript, Python, Ansible, Git, and many more tools.
